About the Edmonton Valley Zoo

The Edmonton Valley Zoo (EVZ) is an accredited facility located in Alberta, Canada. Since its opening on July 1, 1959, the Zoo has become a cornerstone experience connecting visitors with animals in a space dedicated to conservation and care.

EVZ is accredited by CAZA and is a member of WAZA.

About the Valley Zoo Development Society

The VZDS is a registered charity and non-profit supporting the Edmonton Valley Zoo. The Twitch channel is run by VZDS.

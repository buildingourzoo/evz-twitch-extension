About the Edmonton Valley Zoo

The Edmonton Valley Zoo (EVZ) is an accredited facility located in Alberta, Canada. Since its opening on July 1, 1959, the Zoo has become a cornerstone experience connecting visitors with animals in a space dedicated to the care and conservation of our natural world.

The EVZ is proud to be accredited by Canada’s Accredited Zoos and Aquariums (CAZA), and is also a member of the World Association of Zoos and Aquariums (WAZA).

About the Valley Zoo Development Society

The Valley Zoo Development Society (VZDS) is a registered charity and non-profit that was started in 1988 to ensure the continued development of the beloved Edmonton Valley Zoo.

The StreamingOurZoo Twitch channel and streams are run by the VZDS.
